This is a Python Remake of our classic Snake Game.

Rules:
    *--Easy Mode--*
        Snake Can Pass Through The Walls
        Snake Will Die and It's a Game Over If The Snake Bites It's Own Tail.

    *--Hard Mode--*
        Snake Can't Pass Through The Walls and It's a Game Over If It Touches One.
        Snake Will Die and It's a Game Over If The Snake Bites It's Own Tail.
